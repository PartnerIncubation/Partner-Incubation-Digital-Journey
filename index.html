Month 3 – 6

Execution: Pipeline & Demand Gen

This is where enablement turns into real market execution. The focus shifts from learning to action — supporting you in building qualified pipeline through structured demand generation and outbound execution.

Partners participate in SAP's Digital Selling and Demand Generation framework: a high-touch approach to plan, launch, and execute outbound campaigns with SAP expert guidance.

BDR Attendance is MandatoryThe assigned BDR must complete all workshop sessions and lead outbound campaign execution. Attendance is tracked and a certificate of completion is issued upon successful participation.

Digital Selling Workshop — What to Expect

  • Workshop RegistrationRegister your sales team. BDR participation is mandatory; the full sales team is welcome to join. Register for Digital Selling Workshop
  • Campaign Strategy & ActivationDevelop your outbound campaign strategy with SAP guidance and activate it across your target accounts.
  • Multi-Touch Outbound ExecutionExecute structured multi-touch outbound sequences to generate qualified leads and opportunities.
  • Certification of CompletionUpon successful completion of all workshop sessions, receive an official SAP certificate of participation.
  • R2R Framework (Ready to Revenue)Structured approach connecting demand generation activities directly to pipeline creation and revenue outcomes.

Month 7

Mid-Year Business Review

At the midpoint of the program, a formal Business Review session is organized to review progress, assess execution, and align on priorities for the second half of the journey.

The session brings together your team and relevant SAP stakeholders — including your Incubation Manager and regional Market Unit representatives.

Primary GoalReinforce the main program objective: pipeline generation and first deal closure within 12 months from program start.

Session Agenda (60 Minutes)

  • Partner Presentation – 30 minPresent program progress, GTM activities, pipeline status, and key opportunities under consideration.
  • SAP Review & Expectations – 20 minSAP provides a structured assessment of program progress, delivers feedback, and aligns on second-half priorities.
  • Q&A & Open Discussion – 10 minOpen forum for questions, clarification on next steps, and broader stakeholder alignment.
  • Business Review TemplateUse the structured Business Review template provided by SAP to prepare and document the session outcomes. Download Template

Month 7 – 10

Close Your First Deal

As opportunities mature, the focus turns to converting pipeline into results. This phase provides targeted support around deal execution, registration, and closure.

You may also benefit from optional deal qualification workshops and best-practice guidance, designed to support you through your first SAP deal closures and set the foundation for repeat success.

Accelerated Support AvailableIf a significant or time-critical opportunity arises earlier in the journey, deal support can be prioritized and brought forward. This phase will be tailored to your specific needs.

Phase 6 — Graduation

Graduate and Scale Your SAP Business

The final phase marks the transition from incubation into a long-term SAP partnership. Together with your Incubation Manager, you will review the full journey, results achieved, and overall readiness to scale.

Partners who successfully complete the program receive an official SAP Certificate of Completion — formally recognizing their progress, commitment, and readiness to operate as a capable SAP partner.

Following graduation, and based on successful outcomes, partners are transitioned to the appropriate SAP engagement model, typically including assignment of a dedicated Partner Manager to support continued growth.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the SAP PartnerEdge Incubation Program before getting started.

The SAP Partner Incubation Program is designed for new and existing SAP partners looking to accelerate their go-to-market activities. Both new-to-SAP organizations and existing partners expanding into new solution areas may be eligible. Contact your regional SAP Partner Recruitment team to confirm eligibility.
The program runs for 12 months from the date your partnership agreements are signed. It is structured into 6 phases, each with specific objectives, milestones, and expected outcomes to keep your journey on track.
Your dedicated Incubation Manager is your primary SAP contact throughout the program. They guide you through each phase, help you navigate SAP systems and resources, support your enablement planning, facilitate business reviews, and provide coaching during deal execution.
Development Funds (DF) are SAP-funded investments available to eligible partners to offset the cost of approved enablement activities. Eligibility criteria and the request and claim process are reviewed with your Incubation Manager during Phase 2. Not all partners are eligible; specific conditions apply.
Yes — BDR (Business Development Representative) attendance and completion of all Digital Selling Workshop sessions is mandatory as part of the Incubation Program. The full sales team is encouraged to register, but BDR participation is tracked and required for program completion and certification.
Upon successful graduation, partners are transitioned to the appropriate SAP engagement model. In most cases, this includes the assignment of a dedicated Partner Manager who will take over the day-to-day business relationship and support continued growth beyond the Incubation Program. You will also receive an official SAP Certificate of Completion.
Incubation KPIs are defined at the start of the program in collaboration with your Incubation Manager. They typically cover milestones such as achieving Operational status, completing enablement activities, building a qualified pipeline, and closing your first SAP deal within 12 months. These KPIs define your readiness to graduate.
